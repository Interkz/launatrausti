Methodology

The salary gap is calculated by comparing each company's reported average annual salary (total wage costs divided by number of employees, from annual reports filed with Skatturinn) against the overall average from VR's salary survey (Launarannsoekn), annualized by multiplying the monthly survey average by 12.

A negative gap means the company pays below the VR survey average. A positive gap means the company pays above.

Gap = Company Annual Avg Salary - (VR Monthly Survey Avg x 12) // Gap % = Gap / VR Annual x 100
